Weather Project

The weather website is a dynamic platform designed to provide users with up-to-date weather information effortlessly. Leveraging HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, the website offers a seamless experience for users to access real-time weather data. With a user-friendly search engine, individuals can effortlessly look up weather conditions for any city of their choice. The website not only displays current weather conditions including temperature, wind speed, and humidity but also provides a comprehensive forecast for the upcoming five days. This functionality is made possible through the integration of an API, ensuring accurate and reliable weather updates. Whether planning daily activities or long-term outings, my website equips users with the information they need to make informed decisions.

Movie Project

My favorite movies website is a delightful hub dedicated to celebrating the magic of romantic films. Crafted using simple HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, the website aims to introduce users to the enchanting world of romantic cinema. Romantic movies, often characterized by themes of love, passion, and human connection, hold a special significance in our lives. They inspire, uplift, and remind us of the beauty of relationships and the depth of human emotions. The website serves as a gateway for enthusiasts to explore this genre, providing recommendations and insights into some of the finest romantic movies ever made. At the bottom of the website, users are invited to share their contact details to receive a curated list of 20 of the best romantic movies directly to their email. It's my way of spreading love and joy through the magic of cinema.
